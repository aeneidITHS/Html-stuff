Iconic Magic cards

Black lotus

This card is often considered the most iconic and powerful Magic card of all time. It's an artifact that allows you to add three mana of any one color to your mana pool when tapped. Black Lotus is known for its extreme rarity and game-breaking potential.

Black lotus is famous for being the most expensive magic card due to its rarity(it has only been printed three times,because of the reserved list), you can currently get an alpha Black lotus for $159 999.99 To see more about the card visit: Gatherer

Lighting Bolt

A simple yet iconic red instant that deals 3 damage to any target. Lightning Bolt is celebrated for its efficiency and has been a staple in many aggressive red decks throughout Magic's history.

The art being shown is the one it was originally printed with. It has since been reprinted numerous times with different art made by differnt artist. The artist who drew this card is also the one who drew the original art of the Black lotus card. To see more about the card visit: Gatherer

Gaea's Cradle

A legendary land card that taps for green mana equal to the number of creatures you control. Gaea's Cradle has had a significant impact on decks focused on creature-heavy strategies, particularly in the Legacy and Commander formats.

The only card of the five that was not printed in alpha. It was printed in the set Urza's saga 5 years after Alpha(the first set). It is also the second most expensive after black lotus on this list, this due to the fact that the others have many reprints while Gaea's cradle has not. This due to something called the reserved list. To see more about the card visit: Gatherer

Serra Angel

This classic white creature card has been a symbol of Magic since its early days. Serra Angel is a 4/4 flying creature with vigilance, making it a formidable presence in the game.

The cheapest of the five cards, this due to the fact that it sees the least amount of play and due to the fact that it has a been reprinted regularly. To see more about the card visit: Gatherer

Counterspell

A blue instant that simply counters any target spell. Counterspell is renowned for its efficiency and effectiveness in control decks, making it a staple in many blue-based control strategies.

The original counterspell which has spawned many ititerations and versions of it. Said to be the reason people are afraid of people who play blue lands. To see more about the card visit: Gatherer
